Our history
Like any other unique place, we have our own special history. The idea of the restaurant came to the founders suddenly. While walking through the forest, the owner of our restaurant got stuck hundreds of kilometers from the nearest populated area. Far from civilization and communications, they had to temporarily arrange a simple life for themselves, add and cook their own food.
